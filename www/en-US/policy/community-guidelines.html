Welcome

Image Improv is about playing with friends and family, living in the moment, and taking inspiration from the world around us. Our goal in creating these Guidelines is to accommodate the broadest range of self-expression, while balancing the need for Image Improv-ers to be able to use our services safely and enjoyably.

Here are some important guidelines for using Image Improv.

Adult Content

  • We prohibit accounts that promote or distribute pornographic content.
  • Breastfeeding and other depictions of nudity in non-sexual contexts are okay.

Nudity or Sexually Suggestive Content Involving Minors

  • Never post, save, or send nude or sexual content involving people under the age of 18 — even of yourself.
  • This includes adding drawings or captions to an Image to make it sexual, even as a joke.

Illegal Content

  • Don’t use Image Improv for any illegal shenanigans.

Invasions of Privacy

  • Don't take pictures of people in private spaces, like someone’s home, a bathroom, dressing room, or locker room, without their knowledge and consent.

Threats & Violence

  • Never threaten to harm a person, group of people, or property.
  • Don't post Images of gratuitous violence.

Harassment & Bullying

  • We don’t tolerate bullying or harassment on Image Improv.
  • Don’t Improv with the intention of making someone feel bad. If someone blocks you, it’s not okay to contact them from another account.

Impersonation

  • Don’t pretend to be someone you’re not — including your friends, celebrities, brands, or other organizations.

Hate Speech

  • Don't post content that demeans, defames, or promotes discrimination on the basis of race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender, disability, or veteran status.

Terrorism

  • We prohibit content that promotes terrorism.

Spam

  • We have zero tolerance for spam.

If you violate these Guidelines, we may remove the offending content, terminate your account, or notify law enforcement. If your account is terminated for violating our Terms of Service or these Guidelines, you may not use Image Improv again. Please take these Guidelines seriously and honor them in the spirit in which they are intended. We will do our best to enforce them consistently and fairly, and ultimately we’ll try to do what we think is best in each situation.
